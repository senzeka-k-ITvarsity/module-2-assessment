MONKEY TRAIL

The Monkey Trail was built and designed in partnership with Chase Trails and features technical rock gardens, boardwalks, jumps and drops. The trail finishes with the awesome 'Lower Cliff' descent. Stile Cop features several extreme/ bike park graded downhill trails.

  • 9h00am : OPENING
  • 11h00am : AFTERMOON
  • 14h00am : STARTING TO CLOSE

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail is a series of loop trails throughout 800+ acres of mature hardwood forest at Eddie D Jones Park. Open dawn to dusk every day.
